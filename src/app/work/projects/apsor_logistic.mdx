## Overview
Apsor Logistics Co., Ltd. (APLC) is a professional logistics company offering transport, warehouse, and customs clearance services with a strong focus on safety and reliability. To support its operations, APLC developed ApsorTMS, a real-time Transportation Management System that streamlines internal logistics workflows—from dispatch to delivery—ensuring timely execution and traceability of each delivery.

## Key Features
- 📍 Real-Time Truck Tracking – Monitor truck locations throughout the delivery journey
- ✅ Job Assignment – Dispatch and manage delivery jobs internally
- 🛠️ Truck Inspection Module – Record and track vehicle condition and inspection status
- 📊 Status Monitoring – View live transport status and operational data
- 🔐 Internal Access Only – Secure app limited to Apsor team use

## Technologies Used
- Mobile App: Design with advanced UX/UI, easy for end users using.
- API: Real Time tracking and update status
